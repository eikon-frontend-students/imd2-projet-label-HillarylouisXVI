ABOUT Doechii

Doechii’s 2024 mixtape Alligator Bites Never Heal showcases her refusal to be confined to a single genre or identity. The Tampa-born, L.A.-based rapper/singer seamlessly moves between styles—boom bap, jazz-infused tracks, house, and R&B —demonstrating her versatility and command over her craft.
